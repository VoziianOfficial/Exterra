Cookies are small text files that may be stored on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies may include local storage, session storage, pixels, tags, scripts, analytics tools, and browser-based identifiers.

These technologies can help websites function, remember preferences, improve performance, and understand general usage patterns.

  • To understand general website traffic and usage patterns.
  • To improve website layout, content, speed, and usability over time.
  • To help protect the website against spam, abuse, or suspicious activity.

  • Blocking some technologies may affect forms, navigation, preferences, or website functionality.
