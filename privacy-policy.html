Exterra is an independent siding provider-matching platform. Exterra does not perform siding installation, siding replacement, siding repair, inspections, manufacturing, retail sales, or direct contracting services.

Platform notice

Exterra helps homeowners submit siding-related requests and may help connect those requests with participating providers. Final pricing, scheduling, availability, licensing, insurance, warranties, project terms, and service agreements are provided by participating providers and are not controlled by Exterra.

1. Information We May Collect

When you use this website, we may collect information that you voluntarily submit through request forms, contact forms, or other interactive areas of the site.

  • Name and contact details, such as email address and phone number.
  • Project category, siding service interest, location details, and request description.
  • Communication preferences and any information you choose to include in your message.
  • Technical information such as browser type, device information, IP address, pages visited, and general usage data.

2. How We May Use Information

We may use submitted information to operate the website, review siding-related requests, respond to inquiries, improve the user experience, and support provider-matching activity.

  • To process and respond to request submissions.
  • To help connect siding-related requests with participating providers where available.
  • To communicate about a submitted request or website inquiry.
  • To maintain website security, troubleshoot issues, and improve website performance.
  • To comply with applicable legal, regulatory, or business obligations.

3. Sharing Information With Providers

If you submit a siding-related request, information from that request may be shared with participating providers or related service partners for the purpose of reviewing or responding to your request.

Participating providers are independent from Exterra. Their communications, quotes, schedules, warranties, credentials, service terms, and final agreements are their own responsibility.

4. Other Information Sharing

We may also share information with vendors, hosting providers, analytics providers, security tools, form-processing tools, legal advisors, or other service providers that help operate this website and support business functions.

We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, security needs, fraud prevention, enforcement of our terms, or protection of rights, users, providers, or the website.

This website may use cookies, local storage, analytics tools, or similar technologies to support core website functionality, improve performance, remember user preferences, and understand general website usage.
